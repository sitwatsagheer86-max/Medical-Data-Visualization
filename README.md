The dataset contains information about 200+ hospital patients with the following attributes:

Patient ID: Unique patient identifier

Department: Medical department (Cardiology, Gastroenterology, Neurology, Oncology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics)

Treatment Type: Type of treatment received (Medication, Observation, Therapy, Surgery)

Doctor Name: Treating physician

Gender: Patient gender (Male, Female, Other)

Age: Patient age in years

Treatment Cost: Cost of treatment in dollars

Hospital Stay (Days): Duration of hospital stay

Recovery Score: Patient recovery score (0-100 scale)
